Happy Birthday!

my dearest love,

i’ve been known to fill pages upon pages whenever prompted, but today i find myself frozen. there is little in any dictionary that can paint the ink in my heart that spells out your name. i find myself at a loss for words regarding you more than anything else in this world. nonetheless, i will try.

a handful of years ago, you knocked on my door. i’ve felt a hand on my shoulder ever since, toying with my collarbone & vindicating all that is to my life. it held reassurance, love… gestures of affection that warmed my heart with laughter & bliss that i was once a stranger to. strings of avoidance could tug on me, but that hand is always there, holding me in place & reminding me where my love belongs.

i cannot imagine a more fruitful connection, one of true understanding & persistence. my words no longer slip into a well or dry under the scorch of the sun, uttered hopelessly into the air. instead, they cascade from my river to yours, honing the most tenderhearted empathy. i can say i am grateful for you, but that is only because no other phrase undoes the labyrinthine web of feelings i extend towards you. indeed, i am grateful. i am indebted above all, because there is no kindness like yours on this earth & all will fail to repay it. you are a blessing, in my world & to this lifetime, & i am beyond lucky to watch a blessing so profound grow another year older. happy birthday.

i love you most ardently,
